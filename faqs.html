How can I schedule an eye screening with Indo Eye Care Foundation?

Simply contact our clinic, and our team will assist in arranging your free eye screening appointmen.

Are all eye surgeries provided by the foundation free of charge?

Yes, Indo Eye Care Foundation offers free cataract and pterygium surgeries to eligible individuals seeking visual improvement.

What types of eyeglasses are available through the free eyeglasses program?

Our free eyeglasses program provides a range of prescription glasses, ensuring personalized solutions for clear and comfortable vision.

How can I support or donate to Indo Eye Care Foundation's initiatives?

You can contribute through our website, participate in fundraising events, or contact us directly to explore various donation options.

What other services, apart from surgeries, does the foundation offer?

In addition to surgeries, Indo Eye Care Foundation provides free medicines, comprehensive eye care, and educational programs to promote lasting vision health

Is the free eye screening only for specific age groups?

No, our free eye screening is open to individuals of all ages to ensure early detection and care for various eye conditions

How long does it take to receive free eyeglasses after the screening?

The turnaround time for receiving free eyeglasses is typically prompt, and our team will guide you through the process..

Are there income eligibility requirements for accessing the foundation's services?

Indo Eye Care Foundation aims to serve everyone; eligibility criteria ensure services reach those with limited access to eye care resources.

Can I volunteer with the foundation to support its mission?

Yes, we welcome volunteers! Contact us to learn about opportunities and contribute to our vision health initiatives.

How does the foundation ensure patient confidentiality and privacy?

Indo Eye Care Foundation strictly adheres to privacy regulations, maintaining the confidentiality of patient information through secure and ethical practices.

About

Indo Eye Care Foundation is charitable foundation of the Indian Community to provide free screenings & surgeries of eye related ailments like cataract & pterygium for the benefit of under priviledge and needy people in Nigeria.
